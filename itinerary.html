Germany

Berlin · Mar 12–14

Mar 12 Arrival in Berlin

⭐ Brandenburg Gate

Landmark

Berlin's most iconic symbol — an 18th-century neoclassical arch that once divided East and West. Best at golden hour.

⭐ Reichstag Building

Landmark

Germany's parliament with Norman Foster's famous glass dome. Book a free rooftop visit for 360° views of the city.

Mar 13 History & Culture

🎨 East Side Gallery

Landmark

1.3km of the Berlin Wall covered in murals and street art. The world's longest open-air gallery — powerful and photogenic.

🏛️ Museum Island

Museum Complex

UNESCO World Heritage site with 5 world-class museums on one island. Don't miss the Pergamon Museum and Nefertiti bust.

Mar 14 Local Vibes

🌳 Tempelhofer Feld

Park

A former airport turned into Berlin's coolest park. Massive open runways where locals bike, skate, kite, and BBQ.

🎉 Berghain

Nightlife

The world's most famous techno club in a brutalist power plant. No phones inside. Getting past the bouncer is the real challenge.

🇨🇿

Czech Republic

Prague · Mar 15–17

Mar 15 Prague Classics

⭐ Charles Bridge

Landmark

14th-century Gothic bridge lined with 30 baroque statues. Go at sunrise to avoid the crowds and catch magical light.

🏰 Prague Castle

Landmark

The largest ancient castle complex in the world. St. Vitus Cathedral inside is jaw-dropping. Worth half a day.

Mar 16 Old & New

⭐ Old Town Square

Landmark

Heart of Prague with the famous astronomical clock. Watch it strike the hour — touristy but worth seeing once.

🏗️ Dancing House

Architecture

Deconstructivist building by Frank Gehry — looks like two people dancing. Rooftop bar has great views of Prague.

Mar 17 Night Out

🎉 Karlovy Lazne

Nightlife

5-story nightclub right next to Charles Bridge. Each floor is a different genre. Massive and wild — classic Prague night out.

🇭🇰

Hong Kong

Mar 22–23

Mar 22 Skyline Day

👁️ Victoria Peak

Viewpoint

The best skyline view in the world. Take the Peak Tram up and hit the Sky Terrace at sunset. Absolutely unreal.

⭐ Victoria Harbour

Landmark

Iconic waterfront between HK Island and Kowloon. Symphony of Lights laser show runs nightly at 8pm.

⛴️ Star Ferry

Transport

Running since 1888. Cheapest and best way to see the harbour — a few HKD for a legendary crossing.

Mar 23 Markets & Nightlife

🛒 Mong Kok

Market

One of the most densely packed places on Earth. Night markets, sneaker shops, street food — pure sensory overload.

🎉 Lan Kwai Fong

Nightlife

HK's legendary bar and club district. Narrow streets packed with rooftop bars, cocktails, and late-night energy.

🇻🇳

Vietnam

Hanoi · Ha Giang · Ha Long Bay · HCMC · Mar 27 – Apr 3

Mar 27 Hanoi Arrival

🏘️ Old Quarter

Neighborhood

Chaotic, beautiful, and alive. Motorbikes everywhere, $1 bia hoi, and the best street food in Southeast Asia.

⭐ Hoan Kiem Lake

Landmark

Hanoi's peaceful center. Walk around at dawn with locals doing tai chi. The red bridge to Ngoc Son Temple is iconic.

Mar 28 Hanoi Exploring

🚂 Train Street

Landmark

A narrow street where a train passes inches from cafés twice a day. Grab an egg coffee and wait for the rumble.

Mar 29 – Apr 1 Ha Giang Motorbike Loop 🏍️

📍 Ha Giang City

Town

Starting point for Vietnam's most epic road trip. Rent a bike or hire an easy rider — 3-4 days of pure adventure.

👁️ Quan Ba Heaven Gate

Viewpoint

First major viewpoint on the loop. Mountain pass at 1,500m with views of the Twin Mountains and terraced valleys.

📍 Dong Van

Town

Remote karst plateau town in the UNESCO Global Geopark. Sunday market with ethnic minorities is incredible.

👁️ Ma Pi Leng Pass

Viewpoint

Vietnam's most famous mountain road. Sheer cliffs, the Nho Que River 800m below. You'll pull over every 5 minutes.

📍 Meo Vac

Town

Deep in the loop. Sunday market is authentic — Hmong, Dao, and Lo Lo people trading in traditional dress.

Apr 2 Ha Long Bay

🌿 Ha Long Bay

Nature

1,600 limestone islands rising from emerald water. Overnight cruise is the move — kayaking through caves at sunset.

🏝️ Ti Top Island

Island

Short but steep hike to a panoramic viewpoint over the bay. Beach at the bottom for a swim after.

Apr 3 Ho Chi Minh City

🛒 Ben Thanh Market

Market

Saigon's most famous market. Haggle for everything. Night market outside is even better for street food.

🏛️ War Remnants Museum

Museum

Sobering and essential. Vietnam War from the Vietnamese perspective. Allow 2-3 hours — it stays with you.

🎉 Bui Vien Street

Nightlife

Saigon's backpacker strip. Cheap drinks, loud music, plastic chairs on the sidewalk. Absolute chaos in the best way.

🇹🇭

Thailand

Bangkok · Pattaya · Chiang Mai · Chiang Rai · Koh Phangan · Krabi · Phi Phi · Phuket · Apr 4–20

Apr 4 Bangkok Temples

🛕 Grand Palace

Temple

Bangkok's crown jewel. Dazzling gold spires and intricate mosaics. Dress code enforced — cover knees and shoulders.

🛕 Wat Phra Kaew

Temple

Temple of the Emerald Buddha, inside the Grand Palace grounds. Thailand's most sacred Buddhist temple.

Apr 5 Bangkok Continued

🛕 Wat Arun

Temple

Temple of Dawn on the Chao Phraya River. Climb the steep central prang for riverside views. Stunning lit at night.

🎉 Khao San Road

Nightlife

The legendary backpacker street. Buckets of cheap cocktails, pad thai, scorpion skewers, and pure madness after dark.

Apr 6 Pattaya

🪂 Thai Sky Adventures

Adventure

Tandem skydive over the Thai coast. 13,000 feet, 60 seconds of freefall with ocean views. Bucket list stuff.

Apr 7–8 Chiang Mai

🏯 Chiang Mai Old City

Historic Area

Walled city packed with temples, cheap massages, and amazing food markets. Sunday walking street is legendary.

🛕 Doi Suthep

Temple

Golden temple on the mountain. 309 steps up (or cable car). Panoramic city views from the top.

Apr 9 Chiang Rai Temples

🤍 White Temple

Temple

Surreal all-white temple from a dream. The bridge of rebirth over a sea of hands is unforgettable.

💙 Blue Temple

Temple

Vibrant sapphire blue with intricate carvings. Less crowded than the White Temple — equally stunning for photos.

🖤 Black House

Museum

Dark, eerie art complex. Animal skins, skulls, and surreal sculptures. The anti-White Temple.

Apr 10 Nature Day

🌿 Doi Inthanon National Park

Nature

Thailand's highest peak at 2,565m. Waterfalls, twin pagodas, cloud forest trails. Bring a jacket — it's cool up there.

Apr 11–13 Koh Phangan 🌕

🏖️ Haad Rin Beach

Beach

Home of the Full Moon Party. 30,000 people, neon paint, fire shows, bass until sunrise. Legendary.

🧘 Sri Thanu

Neighborhood

The chill side. Yoga studios, vegan cafes, sunset sessions, and digital nomad vibes.

🎉 Eden Garden Party

Nightlife

Jungle party in the hills. Open-air dancefloor, fairy lights, good house/techno. Magic.

Apr 14–15 Krabi

🏖️ Railay Beach

Beach

Only accessible by boat. Towering limestone cliffs, crystal water, rock climbing. Thailand's most beautiful beach.

🛕 Tiger Cave Temple

Temple

1,237 steps to the top. Brutal climb, incredible payoff — 360° views of Krabi from the summit.

Apr 16–17 Phi Phi Islands

🏖️ Maya Bay

Beach

Made famous by DiCaprio in "The Beach." Turquoise water in a limestone amphitheater. Now has visitor limits.

🌿 Pileh Lagoon

Nature

Enclosed turquoise lagoon surrounded by cliffs. Jump off the boat and float in impossibly clear water.

👁️ Phi Phi Viewpoint

Viewpoint

THE postcard shot. Two bays visible from above. 30-min hike — go for sunrise or sunset.

Apr 18–20 Phuket — Grand Finale

🏯 Phuket Old Town

Historic Area

Sino-Portuguese architecture, colorful shophouses, great street art. Way more character than the beach strips.

⭐ Big Buddha Phuket

Landmark

45m white marble Buddha on a hilltop. 360° views of the island. Active construction — monks welcome visitors.

🏖️ Patong Beach

Beach

Phuket's party beach. Bangla Road at night is neon-lit madness. Beach by day, clubs by night.

🌅 Promthep Cape

Viewpoint

Phuket's best sunset spot. Southern tip with panoramic ocean views. Perfect way to end the trip.
