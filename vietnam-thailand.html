🇭🇰 Hong Kong
Day 1 · Mon Mar 23
Arrive & Neon Kowloon 📸
Land from Shanghai. Check in near Olympic station. Walk to Mong Kok (1 stop) — Fa Yuen St sneakers, Ladies Market. Then MTR to Quarry Bay for the Monster Building (Yick Fat) — THE Hong Kong photo, shoot wide looking straight up before sunset. Evening: Temple Street Night Market for neon street food shots, then Tim Ho Wan for the world's cheapest Michelin dim sum. End at TST waterfront for skyline at night.
🏙 Skyline🥟 Dim Sum📸 Monster Building
🏨 Book HK Hotel
Day 2 · Tue Mar 24
HK Island Golden Hour Circuit 📸
Early: Victoria Peak via Peak Tram (first tram 7:30 AM, beat crowds). Shoot from Lugard Road walking path — no glass, better angle. Midday: Man Mo Temple — incense coils with light beams cutting through smoke, THE shot. Use slow shutter for smoke movement. Lunch: Yat Lok roast goose ($8 Michelin plate). Afternoon: PMQ + Hollywood Road street art. Golden hour: Star Ferry crossing at sunset — shoot from the boat. Night: Lan Kwai Fong bar hop or Ozone Bar (118th floor, highest bar on earth).
📸 Victoria Peak🕯 Man Mo Temple🍗 Roast Goose🍸 LKF / Ozone
Day 3 · Wed Mar 25
Lantau Island → Horse Racing 🐎📸
Morning: MTR from Olympic to Tung Chung (20 min direct). Ngong Ping 360 cable car to Big Buddha — shoot from the bottom of the stairs looking up for scale. Po Lin Monastery. Afternoon: Bus to Tai O fishing village — stilt houses over water, drying fish, weathered faces. Documentary photography gold. Use Classic Chrome or Acros B&W on Fuji. Evening: Happy Valley Racecourse for "Happy Wednesday" night races. Gates 5:15 PM, first race 7:15 PM. Entry $10 HKD (~$1.30). Beer Garden section, bet on a lucky number. Lit racecourse surrounded by skyscrapers = incredible photo.
🏛 Big Buddha📸 Tai O Village🐎 Horse Racing
Day 4 · Thu Mar 26
Rock Climbing & Last Night 🧗📸
Morning: Ferry to Tung Lung Island from Sam Ka Tsuen (30 min). Sea cliff climbing above the South China Sea — bolted sport routes 15-25m, beginner to advanced. Insane photo backdrop. Rent gear at Vertical shop in Mong Kok the day before, or join a group via Hong Kong Climbing Facebook group. Afternoon: MTR back, West Kowloon Cultural District (M+ Museum, free), ICC reflection pool at sunset. Evening: Sham Shui Po for authentic street food + neon, or hotpot in Mong Kok. Last walk through the neon streets — shoot everything.
🧗 Rock Climbing🎨 M+ Museum🍜 Street Food
Day 5 · Fri Mar 27
Hong Kong → Hanoi ✈️
Last breakfast: pineapple bun (菠蘿包) + Hong Kong milk tea at any cha chaan teng near Olympic. MTR to Tung Chung → Airport Express (~35 min total). Fly to Hanoi.

Days 3-5 · Mar 29-31
Ha Giang Loop 🏍️
The highlight of Vietnam. 3 days riding through insane mountain passes, rice terraces, and cliff roads along the Chinese border. Ma Pi Leng Pass is one of the most beautiful roads on Earth. Stay in homestays, eat with locals. Unforgettable.
🏍️ Motorbike🏔 Mountains🏘 Homestay

Full day longtail boat tour — limestone karsts rising from emerald water, James Bond Island, kayaking through sea caves, hidden lagoons. This is Thailand's Ha Long Bay but better. Book a small-group tour for ~$40-60.
