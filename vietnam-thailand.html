The highlight of Vietnam. 3 days riding through insane mountain passes, rice terraces, and cliff roads along the Chinese border. Ma Pi Leng Pass is one of the most beautiful roads on Earth. Stay in homestays, eat with locals. Drop-off back in Hanoi.

    Pick up bikes, meet your guide, start riding. Heaven's Gate pass, Lung Cu flag tower (northernmost point of Vietnam, Chinese border). Homestay in Dong Van📸 Endless mountain vistas. Shoot from the back of the bike or at every stop. Classic Chrome for the greens and mist
  • Day 2 — Apr 2Dong Van → Ma Pi Leng → Meo Vac

  • Day 3 — Apr 3Meo Vac → Du Gia → Ha Giang → Drop-off in Hanoi
    Waterfall stops, rice terraces, local markets. Tour drops you back in Hanoi by evening💡 Bring cash — ATMs are scarce on the loop. Homestays are basic but authentic. Embrace it

Day 12 · Apr 7
Chiang Rai Day Trip — White Temple 🤍
Day trip to Chiang Rai (~3hrs by bus). The legendary White Temple (Wat Rong Khun) — one of the most stunning buildings in Asia. Blue Temple, Black House museum. Back to Chiang Mai by evening.
🤍 White Temple💙 Blue Temple📸 Architecture
  • 6:30 AMBus to Chiang Rai
    Green Bus from Arcade Bus Station (~$5, 3 hrs). Or book a private driver/tour (~$30-40 for the day)💡 The bus is comfortable and cheap. Book at the station or via 12Go app
  • 10:00 AMWhite Temple (Wat Rong Khun) 🤍
    Otherworldly. All-white temple with mirror glass — looks like it's made of ice. Creepy hell bridge, golden toilet building, and incredible murals inside📸 THE shot of Thailand. Bright sunlight makes it glow. Shoot reflections in the glass. Velvia for the whites and sky
  • 12:00 PMBlue Temple (Wat Rong Suea Ten)
    Electric blue interior with a giant white Buddha. Newer and less famous but equally stunning photos📸 The blue interior is insane. Classic Chrome to bring out the contrast
  • 1:00 PMLunch in Chiang Rai
    Local northern Thai food — sai ua (sausage), khao soi, or street food near the clock tower
  • 2:30 PMBlack House (Baan Dam Museum)
    Bizarre and incredible — 40+ dark wooden buildings filled with animal skulls, skins, and art. The "anti-White Temple"📸 Dark and moody. Eterna film sim. Strong contrast between light and shadow
  • 4:00 PMBus back to Chiang Mai
    3 hrs back. Arrive by 7 PM. Grab dinner, Thai massage ($5/hr), early night — fly to Phuket tomorrow
